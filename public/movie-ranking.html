The Best Movies According to Miles

My top 3 movies of all-time

Avengers: Endgame

The Avengers series was my favorite growing up because I was always
a huge marvel fan! This was the ending of the movie series.

Top Gun: Maverick

This movie was very well put together and a great branch off from the
original Top Gun movie.

Whiplash

This movie was a rollercoaster of emotions. The way they portrayed his harsh
environment of his journey to become a face in the jazz drummer world was insane.
